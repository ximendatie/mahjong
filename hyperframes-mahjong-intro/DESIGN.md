# Mahjong Intro Video Design

## Style Prompt

A premium tech-noir product film for mahjong, inspired by high-end SaaS launch reels, Raycast-style kinetic UI teasers, Apple-style continuous zoom transitions, and modern motion galleries. The film should feel like one continuous camera pass through a desktop command center: Mahjong tiles fly through 3D space, provider nodes assemble into a graph, HUD cards snap into a board, and the final red tile locks the brand into memory. Copy is sparse; motion and assets carry the story.

## Colors

- Deep jade: `#082c26`

- Warm foreground: `#f7f3ea`
- Muted foreground: `#aeb9ad`
- Electric jade: `#00d6a3`
- Coral signal: `#ff5a47`
- Gold signal: `#d8a73f`
- Cool indigo: `#7fa5ff`

## Typography

- Display: platform `ui-serif` with `serif` fallback.
- UI labels: platform `ui-sans-serif`, `system-ui`, and `sans-serif`.
- Technical numbers: platform `monospace`.

## Motion Rules

- Use camera-like transitions: zoom-through, directional blur, and glass-slab cover wipes.
- Keep one persistent language: cards, tiles, and HUD panels move as physical surfaces with perspective.
- High-frequency detail lives in secondary motion: scan beams, line traces, tile parallax, and soft glow pulses.
- Entrances must use varied transforms: z-depth, rotateX/Y, clip-path reveals, scale, and staggered opacity.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use the README/showcase screenshot.
- Do not use long paragraphs.
- Do not use generic stock visuals.
- Do not imply mahjong controls other apps.
- Do not use purple-blue gradient defaults or flat page-slide transitions.
